Skywalk’s Documentation!

Note

SkyWalk is a tool that is developed to help pilots become more aware of the various aspects of their flight plan as it can address situations that may need extra attention.

SkyWalk can be found at: https://skywalk.streamlit.app/

SkyWalk contains the following functionalities:

  • Retrieves aerodrome information.

  • Retrieves METAR information.

  • Retrieves NOTAM information.

  • Visualizes and analyzes the weight and balance envelope for your aircraft.

  • Analyzes the required runway length based on the POH, weather conditions in combination with runway surface, angle, etc.

  • Visualizes and analyzes the wind to demonstrate possible critical situations.

  • Analyzes fuel consumption.

  • Shows your entire flight on a map with all relevant information such as METAR, NOTAMs, airspaces, and nearby aerodromes.

  • Analyzes flight time, remaining maintenance hours, and costs based on the Tacho/Tobbs information.

  • Provides a customized checklist with mandatory and practical items needed before and during the flight.

  • Autogenerates ATC Transcripts based on your flight details.

  • Summarizes all details.

  • Increases your situational awareness.
